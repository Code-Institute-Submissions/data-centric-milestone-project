Description

The North is one of the constituent regions of the Seven Kingdoms. It was formerly a sovereign realm known as the Kingdom of the North before the Targaryen conquest and later during the War of the Five Kings and Daenerys Targaryen's invasion of Westeros. Along with the Vale of Arryn, it currently recognizes Daenerys as its sovereign, although it continues to be ruled from Winterfell by House Stark.
